      ABOUT JUMBOKING

      Jumboking is an Indian chain of Quick Service Restaurants (QSR) specializing in vegetarian burgers, co-founded by Dheeraj and Reeta Gupta in 2001.

      They offer a range of Indian-flavored vegetarian burgers, alongside shakes, fries, and ice cream, and have expanded to over 180 franchised outlets across cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru.

      Jumboking has a strong focus on standardized quality and serves as a pioneer in India's franchised QSR sector.
